Frequently Asked Questions

What makes YSEC safe to use?

The YSEC platform will operate via smart contracts that don't have authority over the funds, only the instigator of the pre-sale will be able to interact with their locked funds as determined in the presale.

Why use YSEC?

At this moment there are no working platforms or solutions that do what we want to create. There are parts of our services that are currently existing such as Bounce.finance for pre-sale, team.finance and unicrypt.network which are liquidity / asset locking solutions. Our platform will combine different parts of these existing services into one. So it’s a one stop place to host your own presale, determine which funds / tokens will be locked for how long + liquidity locking.

Will YSEC have governance?

There will be no actual governance within the YSEC platform. Anyone can set up their pre-sale on our platform. You decide whether or not to invest into that project, we will always advice to #DYOR.

What makes YSEC unique?

By combining features such as Liquidity locking, token/asset locking, and presales, all in one platform a much safer investment can be guaranteed. An additional benefit of our platform will be the transparency, investors will be able to make their own assessment of the presale and determine the risks much better.

What security measures does YSEC take?

Apart from liquidity locking, The projects on the YSEC platform are vested by smart contracts. By being able to lock different portions for different lengths of time the whole process of vesting will be transparent to everyone. This will make it less to not attractive for scammers to use the YSEC platform. With this transparency and knowledge investors will be able to make their own assessment of the presale and determine the risk much better.

How to earn ETH from the future YSEC dividend pool.

YSEC will ask for a flat 5% fee of the total ETH raised (subject to change) for using the YSEC platform. 50% of the fees earned will go into the dividend staking pool where token holders can stake their YSEC to earn some ETH. This also means that the more developers use the YSEC platform the more ETH can be earned by our stakers.
